Watch More Movies

By Celeste Olivarez

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

Director: Peter Jackson

Rating: 5/5

About: The future of civilization rests in the fate of the One Ring, which has been lost for centuries. Powerful forces are unrelenting in their search for it. But fate has placed it in the hands of a young Hobbit named Frodo Baggins (Elijah Wood), who inherits the Ring and steps into legend. A daunting task lies ahead for Frodo when he becomes the Ringbearer - to destroy the One Ring in the fires of Mount Doom where it was forged.

Why I Love This Movie

A movie I grew up loving and watching over and over. A connection I have with my dad and brother. Every time I watch it, I appreciate all the real practical sets and makeup used on the vast races of goblins and hobbits. The music by Howard Shore pairs beautifully with the gorgeous landscape shots all across the New Zealand countryside and mountains. A true hero journey from beginning to end.
