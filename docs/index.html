By Celeste Olivarez

Why Are Movies so Important?

Movie theatres around the country are shutting down, and it's sad to see the shared experience of getting together to watch a movie die. Movies have and will always be more than just entertainment for me. I love movies because it's a way to connect with emotion, memory and perspective through picture and words. I love that feeling of getting so invested in a story, of seeing the world through someone else’s lens. Movies create moments that stick with you forever, and there’s something so special about going out to a theater to watch them. Sitting in the dark, surrounded by many strangers all experiencing the same thing at the same time is so crucial to a movie experience. It’s a shared experience that reminds me why storytelling truley matters. In a world that moves fast, movies slow us down, pull us in, and invite us to feel something real.
