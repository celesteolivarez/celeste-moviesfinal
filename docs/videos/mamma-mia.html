By Celeste Olivarez

Mamma Mia!

Director: Phyllida Lloyd

Rating: 5/5

About: Donna (Meryl Streep), an independent hotelier in the Greek islands, is preparing for her daughter's wedding with the help of two old friends. Meanwhile Sophie, the spirited bride, has a plan. She secretly invites three men from her mother's past in hope of meeting her real father and having him escort her down the aisle on her big day.

Why I Love This Movie

This is a movie that holds a special place between me and my mom. She showed me this movie for the first time 15 years ago. I love the mother and daughter duo in this film and it reminds me of the relationship between my mother and I. She is my best friend that tries to understand me, laughs with me and gets on my nerves. Mamma Mia! will forever make me grateful for my mom. This movie has such a funny story line and the music is so fun. ABBA has become a stable roadtrip band to play.
